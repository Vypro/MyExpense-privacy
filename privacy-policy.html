Privacy Policy – MyExpense / Keuanganku

Effective Date: 2025

1. Information We Collect

MyExpense / Keuanganku does not collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information. All financial data including expenses, income, savings, categories, and application settings are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

2. How We Use the Information

The locally stored data is used only for essential in-app functionalities such as:

  • Displaying and calculating your financial summaries
  • Managing expenses, income, and savings records
  • Providing budgeting and analytics features

We do not share, upload, or sell your information to any third parties.

3. Data Security

Your data is stored solely on your device. While we apply standard protections through local storage mechanisms, users are responsible for securing their device, enabling screen lock, preventing unauthorized access, and backing up data as needed.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may utilize Flutter plugins such as Shared Preferences for local data storage. These services operate locally and do not collect, transmit, or share user data. The App does not include advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or any form of external data collection components.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages. No personal data from children is collected, stored, or transmitted.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most recent version will always be available on this page.
